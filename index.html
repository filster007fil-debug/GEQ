FAQ

Here you will find answers to the most popular questions of our customers.

How many companies will contact me?

To ensure you get the best value without being overwhelmed, we typically match you with 1–3 vetted local epoxy contractors. This allows you to compare different garage floor coating systems, warranties, and pricing models. By connecting you with a select few top-rated installers, we encourage competitive bidding while respecting your privacy and time.

How fast will I get quotes?

We know you're eager to start your project. Most of our local flooring pros respond within 24 hours, and many provide same-day estimates depending on your ZIP code. Once you submit your details, our system immediately alerts the epoxy garage floor specialists in your area to ensure you get a fast, accurate quote for your flooring renovation.

Do I pay GetEpoxyQuotes for the flooring service?

No, our matching service is 100% free for homeowners. You pay the contractor you choose to hire directly. While payment terms vary by company, most of our local pros accept major credit cards, checks, and electronic payments.

Is it free to request quotes?

Absolutely. Our service is 100% free for homeowners and business owners looking for professional garage floor coatings. There are no hidden fees or credit card requirements to use our platform. We simply connect you with top-rated local epoxy flooring installers so you can compare competitive prices and find the best fit for your budget, with no strings attached.

What affects the price the most?

Several key factors determine the final epoxy flooring cost. The primary drivers include the total square footage of the area, the current condition of your concrete surface (specifically if it requires extensive crack repair or diamond grinding), and the type of finish you select. For instance, a basic solid color epoxy will differ in price from a multi-layer decorative flake system or a high-end metallic finish.

Do you offer epoxy and polyaspartic options?

Yes! Our network of local contractors specializes in a wide variety of high-performance systems. Whether you are looking for a classic residential epoxy floor or a heavy-duty polyaspartic coating known for its UV resistance and rapid cure time, we've got you covered. During your consultation, your matched pro will explain the benefits of epoxy vs. polyaspartic to help you choose the most durable floor coating for your garage, basement, or industrial workspace.
