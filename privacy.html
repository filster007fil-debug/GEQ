2. What Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

A. Information you provide to us

When you request quotes or contact us:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • ZIP code / city / service location
  • Project details you enter (e.g., type of floor, size, timeline, preferences)
  • Any other information you choose to share in free-text fields or messages

B. Information collected automatically

When you browse the Site:

  • IP address and general location (city/state level)
  • Device type, browser type, operating system
  • Pages viewed, time on site, referring/exit pages
  • Clicks and other on-site interactions
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 6)

C. Information from partners or service providers

In some cases, we may receive information from analytics, advertising, or form/automation providers to help operate, secure, and improve the Site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Provide the Services (e.g., process your request and facilitate quote matching)
  • Share your request details with relevant flooring contractors/providers (when you submit a quote request)
  • Communicate with you about your request (confirmations, clarifications, updates)
  • Improve and optimize the Site, user experience, and lead quality
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud, and enforce our terms
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes
  • Measure marketing performance (e.g., conversions, calls, form submissions)

4. How Quote Requests Work (Lead Sharing)

If you submit a quote request through the Site, you understand that:

  • Your request information may be shared with independent third-party contractors/service providers so they can review your project and contact you with quotes or questions.
  • Those contractors/providers may contact you by phone call, text message, or email based on the information you provide.
  • Contractors/providers are responsible for their own services, pricing, and communications. We do not perform the flooring service ourselves unless explicitly stated on the Site.

Important: We aim to share your request only with relevant providers, but the exact number and response timing can vary by location and availability.

5. When We Share Information

We may share information in the following situations:

A. With contractors/service providers

To deliver quotes and fulfill your request (as described in Section 4).

B. With service providers ("processors")

We use vendors to run our Site and operations (e.g., hosting, analytics, form tools, call tracking, email delivery, security). They may process information on our behalf under contractual obligations.

C. For legal and safety reasons

We may disclose information if required to comply with law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect our rights, users, and the public (including fraud prevention).

D. Business changes

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or asset sale, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not sell personal information for money in the traditional sense. However, sharing quote-request information with contractors/providers is a core part of how the Services work.

We may use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to:

  • Keep the Site working properly
